Use Your Band Properly

↔️
More Tension
Step farther on band to increase resistance.
⏬
Slow Reps
3 seconds on the way down for every rep.
⏸️
Hold at Top
2-second squeeze at peak contraction.
🔢
High Reps
Stay in the 12–20 rep range for toning.
Avoid — keeps legs slim
  • Heavy squats
  • Heavy lunges
  • High-load leg press
Focus on — builds glutes
  • Hip thrust
  • Kickbacks
  • Bridges
  • Romanian deadlift
