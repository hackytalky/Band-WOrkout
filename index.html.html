Key Reminders
These make the difference between okay results and amazing results.
🐢
Slow reps
3 seconds on the way down. Control every rep — don't let momentum do the work.
💫
Squeeze at top
Hold 2 seconds at peak contraction. This is where the magic happens for glutes and shoulders.
🔥
Go near fatigue
Last 2 reps should be challenging. If it's easy, use a stronger band or add a rep.
⏱️
Short rest
Keep rest at 30–45 seconds between sets to maintain intensity and burn.
📈
Progressive overload
Every 2 weeks, add reps or switch to a heavier band to avoid plateaus.
💧
Stay hydrated
Drink water before, during, and after. It affects performance and recovery.

The formula that works

✓ Slow reps (3 sec down) + squeeze at top (2 sec)
✓ Go near fatigue every set
✓ Short rest (30–45 sec) between sets
✓ Stay consistent — 4 days, every week
